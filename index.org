* About Me

I received the Master's degree in the Information and Computer
Sciences from Kwansei University, Hyogo, Japan, in 2019.  I'm a member
of IEEE and IEICE.

I'm currently a software enginner at NTT Communications in Japan
from 2019.  I develop and maintain a WebRTC platform SkyWay which
enables real-time communication on the web easily without setting up
and operating servers normally required for WebRTC.

My interests are ...
- Modeling and analysis of communication networks
- Programming (JavaScript, Python, Mathematica)
- Gaming (League of Legends, FPS)
